Favourite Pokemons

Absol

Brief Explanation why

I think this one is more along the line of love at first type sort of thing. Like when I first saw the pokemon I instantely knew that this one is my favourite pokemon. I like the design of the pokemon a lot, and the shint is pretty unique as well. Then there is the lore aspect about the pokemon which makes it even cooler.

Favourite Pokedex Entries

Game Entry
Ruby Every time ABSOL appears before people, it is followed by a disaster such as an earthquake or a tidal wave. As a result, it came to be known as the disaster POKéMON.
FireRed/LeafGreen It appears when it senses an impending natural disaster. As a result, it was mistaken as a doom-bringer.
Emerald It sharply senses even subtle changes in the sky and the land to predict natural disasters. It is a long-lived POKéMON that has a life-span of 100 years.
Diamond/Pearl It senses coming disasters and appears before people only to warn them of impending danger.
Ultra Sun (Mega(one with wings))Normally, it dislikes fighting, so it really hates changing to this form for battles.

Favourite Pokedex Entries

Game Entry
Ruby FLYGON is nicknamed “the elemental spirit of the desert.” Because its flapping wings whip up a cloud of sand, this POKéMON is always enveloped in a sandstorm while flying.
Sapphire FLYGON whips up a sandstorm by flapping its wings. The wings create a series of notes that sound like singing. Because the “singing” is the only thing that can be heard in a sandstorm, this POKéMON is said to be the desert spirit.
